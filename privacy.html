Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 5, 2026 · Applies to the Cocktella iOS app

Cocktella is built to work without an account and without tracking. This policy explains the little data we do handle, why, and how you can delete it.

Using Cocktella as a guest

You can use the entire core app — browsing recipes, your bar cabinet, favorites, shopping list, custom recipes, and your "I made this" journal — without an account. In guest mode all of that data stays on your device only. We receive nothing.

If you create an account

Accounts exist to sync your data across devices and to take part in the community. When you sign up we store:

  • Email address and password (passwords are hashed; we never see them)
  • Profile details you choose: display name and optional profile photo
  • Your synced app data: bar cabinet, favorites, shopping list, custom recipes, and cocktail journal
  • Your community content: recipes you publish, ratings & reviews you write, and reports you submit

Published recipes and reviews are visible to other users together with your display name and profile photo — never your email address.

Feedback

If you use "Send Feedback" in the app, we receive your message, an optional screenshot you attach, and recent technical logs from the app to help us reproduce the problem.

Crash reports

The app uses Sentry to report crashes and errors so we can fix them. Crash reports contain technical information (device model, OS version, app version, what the code was doing when it failed). We configure Sentry to not collect personal data, and we do not use it for analytics, advertising, or session tracking.

What we don't do

  • No advertising and no ad networks
  • No selling or sharing of your data with data brokers
  • No behavioural analytics or cross-app tracking
  • No location access, no contacts access

Where your data lives

Account data and community content are hosted by Supabase (our database and authentication provider). Crash reports are processed by Sentry. Both act as data processors on our behalf.

Content moderation

Community content can be reported by any user. Our moderators review reports and may hide or remove content that violates the Terms of Use. Moderation actions are logged.

Deleting your data

You are in full control:

  • In the app: Profile → "Delete all my data" permanently deletes your custom cocktails, favorites, cabinet, shopping list, journal, and — if you're signed in — your cloud data and your account itself.
  • By email: contact us at the address below and we'll delete your account and data.

Age

Cocktella is about alcoholic drinks and is intended only for people of legal drinking age in their country. It is not directed at children.

Changes

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, we'll note it in the app's release notes and update the effective date above.
